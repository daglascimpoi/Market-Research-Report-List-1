<p><ul><li>Digital Oscilloscope</li><li>Analog Oscilloscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-Purpose Oscillograph market is primarily divided into two types: digital oscilloscopes and analog oscilloscopes. Digital oscilloscopes capture and display waveforms using digital technology, offering advanced features such as signal processing, storage, and connectivity options for data analysis. In contrast, analog oscilloscopes utilize traditional cathode-ray tube technology to provide real-time waveform visualization without digital processing. While digital oscilloscopes are favored for precision and versatility, analog oscilloscopes remain valued for their simplicity and ability to display continuous signals directly.</p></p>
