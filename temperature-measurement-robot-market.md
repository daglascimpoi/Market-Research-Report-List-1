<p><p>The temperature measurement robot market is primarily categorized into two types: infrared imaging and thermal imaging. Infrared imaging utilizes sensors to detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, providing real-time temperature readings without contact. This technology is ideal for quick assessments in dynamic environments. Thermal imaging, on the other hand, creates detailed visual representations of temperature variations across surfaces, enabling analysis of heat patterns and anomalies. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in applications like industrial monitoring, healthcare, and safety inspections.</p></p>
