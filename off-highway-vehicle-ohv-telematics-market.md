<p><strong>The Off-Highway Vehicle (OHV) Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellular-Based OHV Telematics</li><li>Satellite-Based OHV Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Off-Highway Vehicle (OHV) telematics market is primarily categorized into two types: cellular-based and satellite-based telematics. Cellular-based OHV telematics utilizes mobile networks to transmit data, offering real-time tracking and communication capabilities within areas covered by cellular service. In contrast, satellite-based OHV telematics relies on satellite technology for global coverage, making it ideal for remote locations where cellular signals are unavailable. Both types provide valuable insights into vehicle performance, location, and operational efficiency, enhancing fleet management and safety.</p></p>
