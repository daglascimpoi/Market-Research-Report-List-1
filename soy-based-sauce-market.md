<p><ul><li> Brewed Soy-based Sauce</li><li>Blended Soy-based Sauce </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy-based sauce market primarily comprises two types: brewed soy-based sauce and blended soy-based sauce. Brewed soy-based sauce is traditionally made through a fermentation process, resulting in a rich, complex flavor profile. In contrast, blended soy-based sauce combines brewed sauce with additional ingredients like hydrolyzed vegetable protein, offering a quicker production method and a milder taste. Both types cater to diverse culinary preferences and applications, with distinct uses in various cuisines, enhancing dishes with umami and savory notes.</p></p>
