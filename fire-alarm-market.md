<p><p>The fire alarm market is categorized into three main types: smoke detectors, flame detectors, and others. Smoke detectors sense the presence of smoke particles to alert of potential fires, using technologies like ionization or photoelectric sensors. Flame detectors identify the infrared and ultraviolet radiation emitted by flames, providing rapid response in environments like industrial areas. The 'others' category includes various fire safety devices such as heat detectors and carbon monoxide detectors, contributing to comprehensive fire protection systems.</p></p>
