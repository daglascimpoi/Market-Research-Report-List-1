<p><p>The flow chemistry reaction system market comprises various reactor types tailored for efficient chemical processes. Continuous Stirred Tank Reactors (CSTR) ensure uniform mixing and temperature control, ideal for larger-scale operations. Plug Flow Reactors (PFR) facilitate high conversion rates in continuous processes, providing better control over reaction times. Micro Reactor Systems (MRT), with their small scale and high surface area, enable rapid reactions and precise temperature management, making them suitable for research and development and specialized applications in pharmaceuticals and fine chemicals.</p></p>
